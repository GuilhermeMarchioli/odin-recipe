Cappuccino

these kinds of beverages are generally made in cafe shops due to the type of instruments required for this recipe. Yet it can be attempted at home with the available kitchen equipment. So in this recipe, I have used a mixer blender to get the same froth and thickness that you get with a coffee machine. Well, to be honest, you may not get the same result, but still worth spending this effort compared to buying it from the cafe.

Ingredients:

  • ½ cup instant coffee powder
  • ½ cup sugar
  • hot milk
  • chocolate sauce

Steps:

  1. Firstly, in a mixer jar take ½ cup instant coffee powder and ½ cup sugar.
  2. Pulse and grind to a fine powder. You can also use hand beater to make coffee.
  3. Scrape in between to prevent heating and uniform grinding.
  4. Continue to pulse and grind until the mixture turns thick and frothy. Also, you can notice the mixture has turned light brown.
  5. You can store the cappuccino mixture in an airtight box for a week in the refrigerator.
  6. To make coffee, take 1 tbsp of prepared cappuccino mixture.
  7. Pour in hot milk and mix well.
  8. You can decorate and design Homemade Cappuccino with chocolate sauce.
