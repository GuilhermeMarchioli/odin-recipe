American Pancake

Easy, American-style, fluffy pancakes are great for feeding a crowd at breakfast or brunch. Top with something sweet like fruit, jam or syrup, or rashers of crispy bacon.

Ingredients:

  • 225g Flour
  • 2 eggs
  • 150 g sugar
  • A pinch of salt
  • 3 tablespoons of sugar
  • 1 packet of vanilla sugar
  • 6 tablespoons of oil
  • 1 glass of milk (about 250 ml)
  • 14 g of baking powder (about 1 packet)
  • Chocolate chips (amount to taste)

Steps:

  1. Prepare the batter: o In a large bowl, whisk the eggs with the sugar and orange zest until fluffy. Add the oil and milk, and mix well.
  2. Combine the dry ingredients: In another bowl, mix the flour, baking powder, and a pinch of salt. Gradually add this dry mixture to the wet mixture, stirring to avoid lumps.
  3. Add the chocolate chips: Add the chocolate chips to the batter and gently fold them in.
  4. Cook the Pancakes: Heat a non-stick pan over medium heat. If necessary, lightly grease with a bit of oil or butter. Pour a small ladle of batter into the pan to form a pancake. Cook for about 2 minutes on each side, or until bubbles appear on the surface and the pancake is golden.
